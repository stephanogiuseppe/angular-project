Joseanne Viana

I am Joseanne Viana a Ph.D. student at University Carlos III de Madrid in the Signal Processing and Communication Department.

I received her Bachelor’s degree in Telecommunication Engineering from the University of Campinas - Brazil. I was an Early-Stage Researcher in the project TeamUp5G, a European Training Network in the frame of (MSCA ITN) of the European Commission’s Horizon 2020. My research interests are wireless communications applied to interconnected systems such as UAVs, aerial vehicles, and non-terrestrial devices.

Currently, I am working in the field of Machine Learning applied in wireless communications, with a specific focus on interconnected systems such as Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs), aerial vehicles, and non-terrestrial devices.
